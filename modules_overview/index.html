Modules

Here is a quick overview of the internal logic of the Galaxy cube :

  • Analog signal Generator : generates an arbitrary 16-bit signal by iterating over different sections (analytically defined 3rd order polynomial).
  • Digital signal generator : generates an arbitrary digital signal by iterating over different sections.
  • Pulse forwarder : generates digital pulses synchronously to a specified signal generator.
  • External triggering : starts, stops or pauses specified signal generator in case of incoming digital or mqtt input.
  • Signal summer : sums different 16-bit signals together, before they reach the DAC.
  • Digital galvo driver : Additional output for the 16-bit signals. Can drive a xxx-xxx galvomiror. Additional custom hardware is needed.
